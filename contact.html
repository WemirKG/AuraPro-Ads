How The Process Works

We keep things simple, transparent, and fully guided—so you always know what’s happening at every step.

Step 1: Share Your Project & Choose a Package
Fill out the short form with your business info, goals, and the package you want. When you submit, you’ll be taken to a secure PayPal checkout to lock in your spot.

Step 2: Aura Reviews Your Details & Designs Your Concept
Our AI project director, Aura, and the AuraPro Ads team review your submission and build a cinematic ad concept tailored to your brand, offer, and target audience.

Step 3: Concept & Next Steps Email
You’ll receive an email that confirms your package, outlines how many videos and lengths you’re getting, summarizes the creative direction, and explains timing and next steps.

Step 4: You Approve & Production Begins
Once you reply with your approval (or small tweaks), your ad moves into production. We generate your AI-powered video ads based on the approved concept.

Step 5: Delivery of Your Cinematic Ads
Your completed ads are delivered digitally and ready to use for social media, paid ads, website promotions, and launches. Just download, upload, and start promoting.
