How The Process Works

We keep things simple, transparent, and fully guided—so you always know what’s happening at every step.

Step 1: Provide Info & Choose a Package
Fill out the short form with your business info, goals, and the package you want. When you submit, you’ll be taken to a secure PayPal checkout to lock in your ad project.

Step 2: Aura Reviews Your Details & Designs Your Concept
Our AI project director, Aura, reviews your submission and turns it into a clear cinematic concept tailored to your brand, offer, and audience based off your responses.

Step 3: Your Ads Enter Production
Once your concept is set, your ad(s) move straight into production.

Step 4: Delivery & Quick Feedback
Your completed ad(s) are delivered digitally and ready to post for social media, ads, website promotions, and launches. With your delivery email, you’ll also receive a quick survey to share feedback and help us keep leveling up AuraPro Ads.
